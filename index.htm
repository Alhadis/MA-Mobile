Biography

One of the first doom metal bands. Influenced by Black Sabbath. Saint Vitus were formed in 1978 under the name Tyrant with Scott Reagers (vocals), Dave Chandler (guitar), Mark Adams (bass), and Armando Acosta (drums), but changed their name to Saint Vitus in 1980.
